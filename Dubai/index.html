    • Dubai

    Dubai is a city located in the United Arab Emirates, in south-west Asia. Dubai is not the capital of UAE however the capital, Abu Dhabi is located about 90 miles away. Dubai is a city jam-packed with attractions, breaking many records. In Dubai you can find the worlds tallest building, amazing architecture, and the worlds largest man-made island. You will find all the information you need to visit Dubai here. I'll be showing you the top 3 attractions in Dubai next. Keep reading!


    3. Palm Island

    Palm Island is the biggest man-made island in the world. It is an iconic and massive palm tree-shaped structure that extends into the Persian Gulf. Palm Jumeirah is one of three planned Palm Islands in Dubai, the others being Palm Jebel Ali and Palm Deira. Palm Island has a lot of luxury spa's, waterparks, and hotels on it! This attraction is a must have because it has amazing landscapes and just looks amazing in general.

    2. Dubai Aquaventure

    Dubai Aquaventure is a waterpark on the Dubai Palm Island, and it is one of the biggest waterparks in the world! Dubai Aquaventure is known for it's thrilling water-slides, that are world famous! A lot of vloggers have visited this park and most of their videos got over 200K views. It's most famous slide, goes through a glass tank full of sharks! Scary!
